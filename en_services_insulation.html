Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) Services

The ultimate thermal insulation and light-weight roofing solution. SPF reduces up to 50% of your energy costs and provides superior durability.

WHAT IS SPRAY POLYURETHANE FOAM?

Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) is a liquid plastic that is sprayed onto a surface where it rapidly expands 20 to 30 times its original liquid volume. It acts as a **thermal insulator, air barrier, and waterproofing layer**, all in one application.

Key Benefits:

  • **Superior Thermal Resistance:** Excellent R-value compared to other materials.
  • **Seamless:** Reduces noise and eliminates air/moisture intrusion via joints.
  • **Long Life:** Can be recoated every 10-15 years to renew waterproofing and UV protection.
  • **Reduces HVAC Costs** dramatically.

WHY CHOOSE SPF OVER OTHER MATERIALS?

Up to 50% Energy Saving

SPF's air-sealing properties eliminate energy loss through gaps, making it highly effective at maintaining a consistent indoor temperature.

Lightweight and Structural

SPF is extremely lightweight, adding minimal stress to the structure, yet provides rigid stability and helps reduce structural damage from high winds.

LEED & Green Construction

It helps conserve energy and assists in obtaining LEED and other green construction credits for sustainable, environmentally friendly buildings.
