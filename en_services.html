High-Performance Polyurea Applications

Our experts apply custom-engineered Polyurea coatings to meet the highest standards of protection, waterproofing, and durability for demanding environments.

What is Polyurea?

Polyurea is a spray-applied elastomer created by the chemical reaction of isocyanate and synthetic resin. It forms a **fast-curing, flexible, and exceptionally durable** waterproof coating that adheres tightly to almost any surface: concrete, metal, wood, and more. This seamless application ensures long-term protection.

1. Thermal Insulation (Polyurethane Foam)

Polyurea foam offers excellent thermal insulation properties, significantly reducing heat transfer and subsequently lowering energy consumption and HVAC costs. Ideal for:

  • Roofs and Cavity Walls
  • Cold Storage Facilities and Warehouses
  • External HVAC Ducts and Equipment

2. Roofing & Waterproofing (Seamless Polyurea)

We apply Polyurea as a high-strength, seamless roofing membrane that offers superior waterproofing and UV resistance, protecting flat roofs and infrastructure from pooling water and extreme climate conditions.

  • Flat Roofs and Industrial Facilities
  • Expansion Joints and Bridge Decks

3. Industrial and Commercial Flooring

Polyurea creates durable, slip-resistant flooring for demanding environments. Its resistance to chemicals, abrasion, and heavy machinery impact makes it perfect for:

  • Workshops, Factories, and Hangars
  • Parking Decks and Ramps
  • Commercial Kitchens and Laboratories

4. Tank Lining and Chemical Containment

An excellent protective liner for storing liquids, offering corrosion resistance and a long service life, even in chemically aggressive environments.

  • Potable Water Tanks
  • Chemical Storage Containers
  • Wastewater Treatment Facilities
